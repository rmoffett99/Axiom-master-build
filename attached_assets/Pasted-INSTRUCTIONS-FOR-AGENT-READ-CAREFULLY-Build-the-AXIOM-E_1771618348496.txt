DETAILED PROBLEMS / PAIN POINTS (VERBATIM-GROUNDED)
====================================================

Core Enterprise Problem:
Decisions are ephemeral and degrade silently, creating massive hidden risk.

In modern organizations, important decisions are made every day, but they are rarely captured properly.
The result is accumulated “decision debt” — the compounding cost of untracked, outdated, or unowned decisions
that turn into operational, compliance, legal, strategic, or reputational risks when no one notices the degradation.

Specific pains that AXIOM directly addresses:

• Important decisions are recorded incompletely or not at all.
  Only the final outcome is noted (if anything), but the full context — rationale, assumptions,
  decision owner, and supporting evidence — is lost over time, leading to institutional amnesia.

• Assumptions underlying decisions are never tracked or monitored.
  Every decision rests on assumptions about market conditions, data, regulations, technology,
  or internal factors. When assumptions change, expire, become invalid, or are disproven,
  the decision silently degrades without visibility or alerts.

• “Decision debt” accumulates invisibly and dangerously.
  Decisions degrade due to changed assumptions, departed owners, overdue reviews,
  or forgotten context — creating real organizational risk that surfaces only during crises,
  audits, or post-mortems.

• No centralized, tamper-proof system of record exists for decisions.
  Decision records are scattered across emails, Slack threads, meeting notes, documents, or memory,
  and can be altered, deleted, or lost — failing audits, regulatory scrutiny, legal discovery,
  board questions, and internal accountability.

• Weak or nonexistent audit trails for decisions.
  When things go wrong, organizations cannot prove who decided what, when, why,
  on what basis, or with what evidence. Institutional memory disappears when people leave.

• Leadership and boards lack visibility into decision health.
  Executives, General Counsel, and Compliance & Risk leaders cannot quickly see degraded decisions,
  accountability gaps, assumption risks, or organizational decision quality without manual investigation.

• The “why” behind major decisions disappears with turnover.
  Rationale and context vanish when teams change, forcing risky re-decisions or perpetuation of outdated choices.

====================================================
EXACT FEATURES AXIOM MUST DELIVER
====================================================

• A structured record of every significant decision, preserving context, ownership,
  assumptions, and supporting evidence for as long as they matter.

• Capture decisions with full context — not just outcomes.

• Track assumptions over time, including valid, expired, and disproven states.

• Detect decision debt before it becomes risk by scoring decision health
  and flagging when assumptions change, owners leave, or reviews are overdue.

• Maintain an append-only, tamper-proof audit trail where every action
  is recorded immutably at the moment it occurs.

• Provide board-ready, read-only executive visibility into decision health,
  risk exposure, and accountability.

====================================================
TARGET USERS
====================================================

• General Counsel — prove who decided what, when, and why with an immutable record.
• Compliance & Risk Leaders — monitor assumption health and maintain audit-ready documentation.
• Executives & Boards — see organizational decision health without losing institutional memory.
