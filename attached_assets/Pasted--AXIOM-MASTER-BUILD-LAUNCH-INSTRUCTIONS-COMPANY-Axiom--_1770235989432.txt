----------------------------------------
NON-NEGOTIABLE PRINCIPLES
----------------------------------------

1) Decisions are APPEND-ONLY
   - No deletes
   - No silent edits
   - All changes create versions

2) Every decision has ONE accountable owner (human)

3) Assumptions are FIRST-CLASS ENTITIES

4) Leadership turnover must not break context

5) AXIOM™ explains decisions — it does NOT manage workflows

6) This is authority software, not engagement software

If unsure → choose the safer, more explicit option.

----------------------------------------
CORE CONCEPT
----------------------------------------

AXIOM™ is the system of record for how a company thinks.

It captures:
- why decisions were made
- what assumptions justified them
- who owned them
- when they must be revisited

It continuously detects decision risk (“Decision Debt™”) when:
- assumptions expire
- owners leave
- time passes without review
- decisions contradict each other
