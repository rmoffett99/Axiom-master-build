# AXIOM Demo Script (Internal Use Only)

**Audience:** Executive, non-technical
**Duration:** 30–45 seconds spoken
**Context:** Default opening for live demos and walkthroughs

---

## Script

"Most companies have a system of record for their finances, their customers, their contracts — but not for their decisions. And that's where risk hides. AXIOM is a system of record for organizational decisions. It captures who made the decision, why they made it, and what they were assuming at the time. Because what happens in most organizations is — someone makes a critical call, six months later the assumptions behind it have changed, the owner has moved on, and nobody remembers the original rationale. When something goes wrong, there's no trail. AXIOM fixes that. Every decision is recorded, every assumption is tracked over time, and the entire history is immutable — so when an auditor, a board member, or a regulator asks what happened, you have the answer. Not from memory. From the record."
